Four Column Layout

The Four Column Layout divides content into four equal quarters.

The Four Column layout is good for galleries or any page that needs to display lists of links or buttons, especially graphical links.

Four Columns can be useful for Home or landing-type pages where several options need to be displayed as a sort of sub-navigation area.

NOTE: Four columns behave differently than other Explore All Column Layouts since they scale to 50% (column halves) at screen sizes of 48em, and then 100% at 25em.
