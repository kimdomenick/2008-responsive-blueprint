Two Column Layout

The Two Column layout divides content into two equal halves.

Two Column layout is good for any page that needs to be divided in half.

The two column layout can also be inserted into the middle of a form to show the form's elements in an inline display on larger screens.

A single column-half div can be used to wrap all of the content in a page, keeping the content contained in the left half of a page. This is useful when there isn't a lot of content and it's visually cleaner than stretching that content the width of the screen.

NOTE: columns only apply to normal to wider screen sizes, since columns scale to 100% once the screen size drops to 48em.
